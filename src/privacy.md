## Introduction

This privacy policy applies exclusively to the SurfScape website. Other products such as Celer, customWin, and Nuage have their own policies in their [respective pages](/products).

## Information we collect

surfscape.eu uses Cloudflare for hosting and telemetry, the only information that is collected through Cloudflare is:

- Number of requests
- Traffic per Country (does not include IP)

Cloudflare Web Analytics is also disabled.

## How that information is used

We use this information to:

- Monitor overall engagement with the website
- Monitor web services usage

We do **not** use this data to track individual users. We will never sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties, except as required by law or to maintain the functionality of the services described above.

## Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to change this policy at any time.
