This page is focused on anyone that wants to help with the development and growth of SurfScape and it's iniatives.

Some of the ways you can help us is by:

- Offering feedback and ideas of our projects (either suggesting a change or coming up with a new idea)
- Fix or implement a feature (geared towards developers)
- Improve documentation (this includes [tidepool](https://tidepool.surfscape.eu) and general code documentation)
- Promote us to other people you know so we can expand our community

## Getting Started

The best way to get involved is by checking our page on GitHub and explore our [active repositories](https://github.com/orgs/surfscape/repositories?q=visibility%3Apublic+archived%3Afalse) and see what can be changed/improved/fixed/added.

One example would be checking [our website repository](https://github.com/surfscape/surfscape) and check for grammar errors.
